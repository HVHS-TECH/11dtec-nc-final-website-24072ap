Alien/extraterrestrial life pretty much means its a living organism that origanted from a planet other than Earth. It can be as simple as bacteria or simple cells. It can also be a plant, animal or something completely different from what we have at Earth. They could just be advanced techonology robots nobody knows. There have been evidence for extraterrestial life on planets. For example on Mars there is a frozen lake or ancient water, on Europa there is subsurface ocean etc.
