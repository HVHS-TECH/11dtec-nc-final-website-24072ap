A planet is a celestial body that is in orbit around the Sun, has enough mass to be roughly round in shape and has significantly more gravitational attraction than anything else near it. The formal definition of what counts as a planet was decided upon by the International Astronomical Union (IAU) in August 2006. But in basic simple words, earth is a planet revolving around a star which is the Sun. However there are also other types of planets like dwarf planets, exoplanets etc. They are still planets but lets say they are differenct species. For example Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune are gas planets or knowns as "gas giants". Dwarf planets usually are almost spherical in shape but they dont have a clear orbit and amy include of asteroids. An exoplanet is basically a planet that exists out of our solar system.
